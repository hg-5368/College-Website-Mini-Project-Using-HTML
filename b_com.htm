B.Com(Honours)

About the Course

Bachelor in Commerce (Honours) or B.Com. Hons. is among the most common undergraduate degree programmes in Commerce. B.Com (Hons.) is a practical curriculum that centres on training students for careers in business and management sectors. This is a conventional marketing undergraduate education. In the final year of the degree programme, applicants will choose specializations to help applicants find job opportunities in their field of interest. B.Com degree programme, in general, is a three-year degree program without any subject specialization. The very same courses, and also a specialization, are covered in B.Com. Hons.B.Com. Hons. is an industry-oriented degree programme that teaches students about business, accounting, marketing, and other related subjects. Candidates who want to work in corporate administration or the business field will greatly benefit from this course. Following this course with a management degree would help students in getting a great job with a decent income.
