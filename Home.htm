About us

Who we are

Saroj Institute of Engineering and Management(SIEM)

Was established in 2003 with a vision to provide well-rounded technical education to students from all over the country. SIEM is currently operating as a joint venture with the Techno India Group (TIG) . The engineering degree institution is approved by AICTE, Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) and affiliated to Maulana Abul University of Technology (MAUT) and offering regular courses in various engineering disciplines. The diploma engineering division is also approved by AICTE and affiliated to WBSCTE. Students at SIEM come from diverse cultures and backgrounds and have been placed in prestigious organizations in various sectors such as IT, infrastructure and Consulting.

We create Nation Builders,not only engineers

The institution is to provide world class technical education to students. The idea is to make SIEM a hub for skill development by strengthening ties with various industries and not just be limited to being a degree giving institution.

We offer undergraduate courses like BCA,B.Tech and BBA,and recently we have launched one postgraduate course MCA. For more details please visit Academics section.
