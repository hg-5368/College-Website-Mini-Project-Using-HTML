Integarted Law Program- BBA LLB

About the Course

BBA LLB is an integrated law course wherein aspirants are taught subjects related to Business Administration as well as Law. As part of a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) LLB course aspirants are first taught Commerce subjects such as Principles of Management, Financial Accounting, Computer Applications, Effective Communication, etc. Thereafter, aspirants are taught law subjects such as Law of Torts, Family Law, Constitutional Law, Property Law, Company Law, Administrative Law, Civil Law, Criminal Law, and the likes. The maximum duration within which an aspirant must complete their BBA LLB course is eight years. As part of an integrated BBA LLB course, aspirants are taught various topics by means of classroom teaching, case studies and moot courts.
