About the Course

Department of Computer Science of SIEM was established in the year 2003 with an objective to promote practical knowledge in latest cutting-edge technologies and research in various domains. Department Laboratories are well equipped with up to date hardware facility with all licensed software and high speed Internet facility. As a student of Computer Science and Engineering, you will undergo through courses covering algorithms, data structures, programming languages, database, network, operating system. You will also get exposure to the state of the art topics like image processing, artificial intelligence, Bioinformatics, computer graphics, computer vision, compiler design, web based application. The department has strong alumni strength of more than 1400+, most of whom are presently employed with the leading corporate like TCS, Infosys, IBM, CTS, Microsoft, Oracle, Cisco, MBT and others. Our team of highly qualified teachers supported by State-of-the-art laboratory equipments, library and classrooms are all hear to ensure that you receive all the very best facilities a student deserves. Our focus is on quality education and we are continuously building our quality to achieve a critical point of success. Our department received grant from MHRD, Government of India through IIT- Bombay to develop highly configured State-of- the-art laboratory at our college is a nodal center of IIT-BOMBAY and IIT-KHARAGPUR.
