About the Course

The B. A. (Hons) Economics programme provides a firm basis for much of the advanced thinking in the Economics discipline. It provides the student with a logical paradigm for modelling and interpreting the behaviour and interactions of households, firms, and government institutions. The programme is consistent with global standards in the Economics discipline. It offers training that is comparable to that of an undergraduate student at the worldâ€™s best universities.
